Privacy Policy — Phyloc

This privacy policy applies to the mobile game Phyloc (“the app”) developed by MonYu Project.

1. Data stored on your device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only (it is not sent to our servers or any third party for our own use):

  • Game progress: total score, last level reached, stars and high scores per level.
  • Settings: thinking time (seconds), grade mode (Junior High / High School), and language preference (if you changed it from the system default).

This data is used only to provide continuity (e.g. “Continue Level X”) and to remember your preferences. You can clear it by clearing the app’s data or uninstalling the app.

2. Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to show banner and interstitial ads. Google may collect and process data (such as advertising ID, device type, and general usage) in accordance with their own privacy policy. We do not control that data collection.

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google’s guide to how they use data in advertising: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

3. No account or personal data

The app does not require you to sign in or create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, or other personally identifiable information.

4. Children

Phyloc is aimed at educational use (e.g. junior high and high school physics). If you are under 13 (or the applicable age in your region), please use the app with a parent or guardian who can agree to this policy on your behalf.

5. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date below will be revised when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the app, you can open an issue or contact the maintainers via the PhyLoc repository (e.g. GitHub Issues or Discussions).

If you contact us, you can request deletion of the email conversation by contacting us at monyuproject@gmail.com.
